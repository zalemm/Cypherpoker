Use the selection box on the left to choose an account for game play,
cash outs, or transfers.

Click on the clipboard button on the right to copy the account address to the
system clipboard. You can use the copied address to check on the status of a
deposit or transfer using a third-party service.

The address displayed is the currently selected and logged-in account.
Click on the clipboard button on the right to copy the account address to the
system clipboard.

This area displays your current account balance.

If a deposit has been made but not yet confirmed, the balance will display 0.
Refresh the page to see the new balance once the deposit is confirmed.

If there's a problem retrieving the account balance it will be "unavailable".

Use this field to enter the password associated with the selected account.

If saving of account passwords is enabled (default), the password for an account
is automatically entered here when the account is selected.

Click on the "USE ACCOUNT" button to use the selected account with the
specified "Password" to create or join a table.

Click on the "CREATE NEW ACCOUNT" to create a new cryptocurrency account
(you will be taken to a new form).

Use this feature to send some or all of the selected account's balance to another address.

Enter the amount to send in the "Amount" field, in satoshis.

Enter the recipient address in the "Address" field.

The final input field (Miner Fee), will be automatically filled with the recommended
optimal miner fee, in satoshis, for the transaction. You can change this value but be aware
that it may affect the time required for the transaction to be included in a block.

When the required information has been entered, click the "CASH OUT / WITHDRAW" button on the left.

Use this feature to transfer some or all of the selected account's balance to another account.

Enter the amount to transfer in the "Amount" field, in satoshis.
Enter the recipient account address in the "Account" field. The account address
must be one created by this account system.

When the required information has been entered, click the "TRANSFER" button on the left.

This form is used to create a new table (game).

As the table's creator you will automatically join it and begin advertising it to other players.

Enter your desired player alias (name) in this field.

Enter a descriptive name for the table (game) in this field.

Enter the required number of players, including yourself, that must join the table
in order for the game to begin.

Enter the required buy-in, in satoshis, for each player joining this table (game).

Once all required players have joined, this amount is subtracted from their accounts
and transferred to in-game balances managed by an associated game contract
until either the hand is completed or a player times out.

When a hand (game) is restarted, this process is repeated.

Enter the amount, in satoshis, to be posted by the big blind for this table.

This amount must be less than or equal to the buy-in.

Enter the amount, in satoshis, to be posted by the small blind for this table.

This amount must be less than or equal to the big blind amount.

Enter the inactivity timeout amount, in seconds, to use for this table.

When a player fails to perform an action before the timeout elapses the game ends and the player is penalized.

Click on this button when all of the above fields have been correctly completed in order
to create the new table and automatically start advertising it to other players.

Once the required number of players has joined the table, the buy-in amount is automatically
subtracted from their accounts and transfered to their in-game balance where it's managed
by the associated game contract.

The contract handles the in-game balances until either a winner(s) is determined or
a player times out.

Enter the desired player alias (name) for the game you wish to join in this field.

Any tables created and advertised by other players will appear below the Join Table header.

Once you've reviewed the information, click on the desired table to join it.

When the required number of players have joined the table, the game will automatically begin.

The advertised buy-in amount will be subtracted from your selected account and
transferred to an in-game balance where it'll be handled by an associated game contract until
the hand (game) has either completed or a player has timed out.

Use the number field on the left to enter an amount to bet or raise, in satoshis, then
click the "BET" button. A bet/raise/call amount is automatically entered for you based
on the current state of the game.

You may fold by clicking on the "FOLD" button when it's your turn.

A new hand may be dealt by clicking on the "NEW HAND" button when the previous
hand has completed and you're the next dealer. The same table parameters as the previous hand
are used to start the new hand.

This area displays a history of completed hands (games).

Winning hand(s) and associated player(s) are displayed in order
from oldest to newest.
